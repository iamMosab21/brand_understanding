Self-Actualization
Personal expression, style as a life project
Hermès • Rick Owens
Customer problem: “I want my story shown through fashion.”
Strategy: Narrative collections, customization, collaborations.
Esteem
Status, prestige, respect
Gucci • Louis Vuitton
Customer problem: “I need to feel respected / superior.”
Strategy: Scarcity, icons, visible branding.
Belonging
Identity, tribe, community
Nike • Supreme
Customer problem: “I want to fit into a group.”
Strategy: Community drops, influencer tribes.
Safety
Reliability, durability, predictable value
Levi’s • Lululemon
Customer problem: “I need something I can trust.”
Strategy: Quality guarantees, warranties.
Physiological
Basic comfort, protection
Hanes • Uniqlo
Customer problem: “I need reliable clothing.”
Strategy: Optimize comfort, durability, price.
